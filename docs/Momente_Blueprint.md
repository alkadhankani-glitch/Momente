# The Momente’ Blueprint

## Product Name
Momente’

## Tagline
Plan. Celebrate. Remember.

## Founder
Alka Dhankani

## Origin Story
Momente’ was born while planning Raja & Anjali’s wedding. What began as a need for simple table lookup and seating management became a broader vision: a Celebration Intelligence Platform that helps people plan, celebrate, and remember life’s most meaningful moments.

## Mission
To remove stress from event planning through beautiful design, thoughtful automation, and intelligent guest experiences.

## Vision
To become the most loved platform for planning and managing celebrations, weddings, corporate events, galas, fundraisers, conferences, birthdays, and family milestones.

## Product Category
Celebration Intelligence Platform.

## Core Philosophy
Every interaction should reduce stress, save time, or create joy.

## Product Pillars
1. Planning — Guests, tables, seating, RSVP, hotels, vendors, budgets, timelines, and event defaults.
2. Guest Experience — Invitations, QR lookup, welcome cards, reminders, check-in, parking, hotels, event details, and guest concierge.
3. Celebration Intelligence — Morning Brief, Celebration Score, Guest Readiness Score, RSVP insights, hotel insights, budget insights, vendor risks, and AI recommendations.
4. Memories — Photos, videos, thank-you notes, guest messages, and post-event memories.

## Version 1.0 — Wedding Foundation
Primary goal: every guest can find their table in under 10 seconds.

## Current Product Decisions
- QR lookup is the flagship Wedding MVP capability.
- Guest import should be simple.
- Capacity belongs to tables only, not guests.
- Meal default for Raja & Anjali: Vegetarian.
- Track only meal exceptions such as Strict Jain.
- Support both digital QR lookup and legacy printed guest cards.
- Bride and groom imports should be simple enough for families to use without training.

## Decision Log
- Capacity removed from guest import to simplify bride/groom side data entry.
- Guest Experience added as a core product pillar.
- QR Lookup prioritized as the live wedding MVP.
- Printed Welcome Cards moved into MVP for hybrid digital + legacy experience.
- Guest Concierge added to roadmap to support guest reminders and readiness.

## Founder Principle
Simple is elegant.

## CTO Principle
